# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Apollonia staff use the private admin area to review reservation requests, confirm or decline dates, and keep the venue schedule accurate. Public visitors use the marketing and reservation pages to understand the venue and submit requests.

## Product Purpose

Apollonia Events provides a calm public reservation flow for a premium private venue and an internal operations surface for managing those requests. Success means staff can quickly trust what is pending, update reservation status without ambiguity, and keep the public experience unchanged.

## Brand Personality

Elegant, calm, and premium. The public site carries the handcrafted Ancient Greek inspiration; admin surfaces should borrow the palette and logo while staying quiet, dense, and task-first.

## Anti-references

Avoid mythology cliches, parchment textures, overused columns, cartoonish Greek motifs, and generic AI-looking luxury. For admin screens, avoid decorative public-site ornament, marketing hero layouts, heavy animation, and restyling the existing public chrome.

## Design Principles

- Keep operations legible before decorative.
- Preserve the public brand without importing public-site ceremony into admin workflows.
- Make every admin action explicit, reversible where possible, and guarded server-side.
- Prefer existing tokens, components, and local patterns over speculative abstractions.
- Keep public pages visually and functionally stable while admin capability grows.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Use semantic HTML, keyboard-operable controls, visible focus states, and text contrast that meets WCAG AA. Respect reduced-motion preferences and do not rely on color alone to communicate reservation status.
